Who am I?

Who is but a function of what, and what I am is a man in a mask ~ V

and that is probably the best line I could use to introduce myself. I don't wear the mask to conceal; indeed, rather ironically, I use the mask to reveal myself. I use it to portray whatever I can understand of myself and those around me. By extension, it is when I wear masks that other people can see themselves in me.
