Padawan Plan

Basic plan:

  • All enrolled students' marks
  • Website-entry for marks
  • Readymade report cards delivered for the first year
  • Automated report cards delivered in subsequent years

Some other cost considerations:

  • Extra cost considerations
    • ₹5000 for the whole school if there are 2 sections per class (KG/Nursery not counted)
    • ₹2,500 for every additional section (3 or more) per class
  • Domain cost
    • Unnecessary if you already have purchased a domain for your school
    • Necessary for any future web endeavours for your school
  • Server Hosting costs
    • Required: Database hosting cost
    • Optional: Deployment Hosting costs (Render/Netlify,etc.)
    N.B. - These costs would be incurred regardless of the service provider, as these are external costs for a third-party

Cost: ₹21,000 ₹18,000 (inclusive of monthly assistance for upto 3 hours to resolve queries for the whole year)
